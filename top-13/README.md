## Why These 13?

### Tier 1: Universal Templates (Top 4)
These four styles are **required knowledge** for any designer. They cover most project types:
- **Minimalism/Swiss** - The default choice for 60%+ of projects
- **Flat Design** - Google's Material Design basis
- **Hero-Centric** - Every landing page template
- **Dark Mode** - Expected feature, not optional

### Tier 2: Modern Standards (5-9)
These represent **current best practices** as of 2024-2026:
- **Glassmorphism** - Apple/Microsoft standard
- **Bento Box** - Clean, organized dashboards
- **Neubrutalism** - The trendy choice for startups
- **Conversion-Optimized** - E-commerce/sales standard
- **Feature-Rich** - SaaS product showcases

### Tier 3: Specialized (10-13)
These serve **specific use cases**:
- **Social Proof** - Trust building
- **Minimal & Direct** - Portfolio/indie
- **AI-Native** - Fastest growing category
- **Soft UI** - Modern alternative to neumorphism
